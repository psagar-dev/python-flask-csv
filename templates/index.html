Sales Dashboard

Analyze sales performance by region, category, and recent orders.

Start the server and view JSON outputs for insights.

Available Routes

  • /sales_trends: Provides sales trends grouped by product category and month.
  • /top_customers: Lists the top 5 customers based on total purchases.
  • /top_regions: Displays the top 5 regions with the highest sales performance.
  • /top_categories: Provides detailed insights into the top 5 categories with the highest sales.
  • /latest_orders: Fetches the 5 most recent orders with customer and product details.
